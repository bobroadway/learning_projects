TwentyTwenty

Image Compare

TwentyTwenty is a plugin for image comparing to make interactive before and afters. Download the zip from the twentytwenty website, and follow the instructions on the website. The short version of those instructions is:

  • place their images in your images folder
  • link to their twentytwenty.css stylesheet
  • link to their jquery.twentytwenty.js script and jquery.event.move.js for mobile touch functionality
  • place two imgs into a div with id, and call the twentytwenty method: 
    • <div id="container1" class="compare">
  <img src="images/blackandwhite.jpg">
  <img src="images/color.jpg>
</div>
            
    • $(window).load(function(){
    $("#container1").twentytwenty();
});
