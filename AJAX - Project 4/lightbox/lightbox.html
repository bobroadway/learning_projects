Lightbox

Image Viewer

Lightbox is a plugin for working with images to create a slideshow and allow them to pop out of their containers. You simply download a zip from the lightbox website, and follow the instructions on the website. The short version of those instructions is:

  • place their images in your images folder
  • link to their lightbox.css stylesheet
  • link to their lightbox.js script
  • use the proper attributes in your html elements
